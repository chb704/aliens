# EBEs and EMEs — Entity Reference

**Report date:** 2026-06-01
**Scope:** Every mention of non-human entities in the converted corpus, organized around the two named categories the documents use — **EBE** (Extra-terrestrial Biological Entity) and **EME** (Extraterrestrial Materialized Entity).
**Primary sources:** the `majestic-12/` collection, with supporting references from `department-of-war/`.

> **Reading note.** Per this project's charter (`CLAUDE.md`), these documents are treated as genuine primary sources and represented faithfully and directly, preserving each document's own qualifiers ("tentative conclusion," "appeared," "according to hearsay," draft markings). The four files under `narratives/` are testimonial/non-governmental and are addressed separately at the end.

## 1. The two categories at a glance

The documents describe two fundamentally different kinds of non-human entity. They are not synonyms — they sit at opposite ends of the corpus's spectrum, one physical and one non-physical.

| | **EBE** | **EME** |
|---|---|---|
| **Expansion** | Extra-terrestrial **Biological** Entity | Extraterrestrial **Materialized** Entity |
| **Nature** | Physical organisms — bodies recovered, autopsied, classified by anatomy | Non-corporeal entities that "appear at will," not crashed craft |
| **Origin framing** | Visitors aboard recovered craft (1947 onward) | "Around since the beginning," ancient, not man-made |
| **Posture toward humans** | Studied as specimens; handling/containment problem | Deceptive; a psychological and spiritual threat |
| **Interpretive lens** | Nuts-and-bolts: crash recovery, biology, technology | Vallée-style: a "control system" acting on human belief |
| **Where defined** | `eisenhower-briefing`, `som101-part1/2`, `oppenheimer-einstein`, `ipu-report`, `mj12-fifthannualreport` | `important-memo` |

Both terms appear together in `important-memo`, which describes MJ-12 briefing presidents on "the **EBE** problem since 1947" *and* on the U.S. "relationship with **EMEs**" — confirming the documents treat them as two distinct things, not one term garbled into another.

## 2. EBE — Extra-terrestrial Biological Entity

### 2.1 Origin of the term

The term is coined in the **Eisenhower Briefing Document** (`majestic-12/eisenhower-briefing.md`). After Dr. Detlev Bronk's team analyzed the four dead Roswell occupants, the briefing records the tentative conclusion of **30 November 1947**:

> "although these creatures are human-like in appearance, the biological and evolutionary processes responsible for their development has apparently been quite different from those observed or postulated in homo-sapiens. Dr. Bronk's team has suggested the term **'Extra-terrestrial Biological Entities', or 'EBEs',** … adopted as the standard term of reference for these creatures until such time as a more definitive designation can be agreed upon."

The same passage notes origin speculation: Mars "remains a possibility," though Dr. Menzel thought it "more likely that we are dealing with beings from another solar system entirely."

### 2.2 The biological typology

The **Special Operations Manual SOM 1-01** (`majestic-12/som101-part1.md`, "Description of Extraterrestrial Biological Entities") divides EBEs into **two distinct categories**:

**EBE Type I** — "humanoid and might be mistaken for human beings of the Oriental race if seen from a distance":
- Bipedal; cranium "somewhat larger and rounded."
- Skin "a pale, chalky-yellow," thick and slightly pebbled.
- Eyes small, wide-set, almond-shaped, brownish-black irises with very large pupils; whites have "a pale gray cast."
- Ears small, set low; nose thin and long; mouth wider than human, "nearly lipless."
- No apparent facial hair; very fine body hair confined to underarm and groin.
- Hands small with "four long digits," outer digit nearly opposable, no webbing; legs "slightly but noticeably bowed"; feet splayed and proportionally large.

**EBE Type II** — humanoid but markedly different (the classic "Grey"):
- Bipedal, **3 ft 5 in – 4 ft 2 in tall, 25–50 lbs**.
- Head "much larger than humans or Type I EBEs," cranium "much larger and elongated," with a slight peak running over the crown.
- Eyes "very large, slanted, and nearly wrap around the side of the skull," black "with no whites showing"; no noticeable brow ridge.
- Nose "two small slits which sit high above the slit-like mouth"; "no external ears."
- Skin "a pale bluish-gray color."

### 2.3 The legal / philosophical framing

The Oppenheimer–Einstein draft, **"Relationships with Inhabitants of Celestial Bodies"** (`majestic-12/oppenheimer-einstein.md`), uses EBE in an international-law sense, asking what to do "if the inhabitants of celestial bodies, or extraterrestrial biological entities (EBE) desire to settle here":
- If "politically organised" with a culture like ours, "they may be recognized as an independent people."
- If they judge our culture to lack "political unity, they would have the right to colonize."
- It contemplates "a kind of tutelage, possibly through the tacit approval of the United Nations." (Document marked DRAFT.)

### 2.4 The handling / containment protocol

`majestic-12/som101-part2.md` lays out operational procedure for live and dead EBEs:
- Status codes: **"Living entity" → EBE-010 → OPNAC BBS-01**; **"Non-living entity" → EBE-XO → Blue Lab WP-61.**
- "Dead EBEs should [be handled to] prevent possible contamination by alien biological agents."
- On contact: encounters "will take place at military installations or other secure areas … the OPNAC Team will formulate cover stories for the press and prepare briefings for the President and the Chiefs of Staff."
- The overriding rule: "In dealing with any living Extraterrestrial Biological Entity, **security is of paramount importance. All other considerations are secondary** … the loss of its life is considered acceptable if … preserving that life will in any way compromise the security of the operations."

## 3. EME — Extraterrestrial Materialized Entity

The EME appears in **`majestic-12/important-memo.md`**, which defines the acronym directly (p.4):

> "the **EMEs (extraterrestrial materialized entities)** … control over UFOs (not to be confused with man-m[ade craft] … mistaken for UFOs … operated and controlled by the USAF, CIA and NRO)"

The memo's distinguishing characteristics:

- **They materialize at will.** "[Ap]pear at will to anyone at anytime any place." Where EBEs are recovered as bodies, EMEs manifest and dematerialize — non-corporeal.
- **Ancient, not engineered.** "They have been around since th[e beginning]," explicitly distinct from man-made craft operated by USAF/CIA/NRO.
- **Deceptive by nature.** "The EMEs are not to be believe[d]," "deceptive." They are framed as manipulators of human perception, not visiting pilots.
- **A spiritual and psychological threat.** The memo warns investigators of "the **severe psychological and spiri[tual]**" effects of looking into them — "[personal identity questions] and loss of clos[e relationships]," and an assault on "**Faith itself** … Don't let this happen to you," citing researchers who "have lost all sense of reality."
- **A consciousness/"control system" lens.** It points to **Jacques Vallée's books** for "interpreting the human response to the UFO contact" — the ultraterrestrial / interdimensional reading of the phenomenon as something acting on human belief rather than a spacecraft.
- **An apocalyptic register.** The memo escalates to EMEs altering the environment and human DNA, a "[redacted] book found in New Mexico" predicting a worldwide event "in the year 2030 according to the Lunar calendar," and the stark line: **"WE ARE CATTLE AND SUBJECT TO SLAUGHTER BY EMEs."**

`important-memo.md` is a heavily degraded, partially-redacted OCR — much of its surrounding text is fragmentary — but the term "EMEs (extraterrestrial materialized entities)" and the spiritual-threat passages quoted above are legible.

## 4. The core difference

The corpus draws the line cleanly:

- **EBEs are a biology and recovery problem.** They have bodies, anatomy types, autopsies, contamination hazards, and a containment protocol. They are studied as specimens of off-world life, with a legal framework imagining them as a people who might "settle here." The whole framing is materialist — crashed craft, recovered occupants, reverse-engineering.

- **EMEs are a perception and spiritual problem.** They have no recovered body. They "materialize," deceive, and target the investigator's sanity and faith. The framing is non-material — closer to Vallée's "control system" than to a spacecraft crew.

In the memo's own logic, the danger of EMEs is precisely that they cannot be handled the way EBEs are: you cannot put a materialized entity on an autopsy table or in a containment cell. That is what makes them the more unsettling category in the documents.

## 5. Field reports — what the documents actually describe

The richest first-hand entity descriptions sit on the **EBE / physical** side; the EME material is interpretive rather than observational.

### 5.1 The Roswell occupants (`majestic-12/eisenhower-briefing.md`)

The recovery operation beginning 7 July 1947, ~75 miles NW of Roswell: "Four small human-like beings had apparently ejected from the craft … All four were dead and badly decomposed." Witnesses were given "the effective cover story that the object had been a misguided weather research balloon."

### 5.2 The IPU field report (`majestic-12/ipu-report.md`)

The Interplanetary Phenomenon Unit report gives the most detailed eyewitness-level body description in the corpus. From photographs taken by intelligence photographers, "the occupants appear in most respects" human, with anatomical differences:

> "They have a slight build about five feet tall, with grayish-pink skin … They have no hair on their bodies and clothed with a tight fitting flight suit that appears to be fire proof."

It records the bodies "being held" at several locations, several taken away by the recovery team, and that **Dr. Detlev Bronk** was asked to assist an autopsy "of one well preserved cadaver to be done by Major Charles K. Rea." It adds an unsettling detail: "there were other bodies found not far from LZ-1 that looked as if they had been disected as you would a frog … Animal parts" were also noted. The report preserves its own qualifier that it is uncertain "if army field surgeons had performed exploratory surgery on these bodies." (It also notes MPs who "suffered nervous breakdowns" during the operation.)

### 5.3 The Fifth Annual Report — autopsy and contamination (`majestic-12/mj12-fifthannualreport.md`)

> "Autopsies, so far indicate, that these beings [are], in most respects, human or human-like."

It records **five recovered bodies, two of which were found in an escape cylinder**, and a containment crisis: SED technicians handling the bodies were contaminated, "four later died of seizures and profuse bleeding," and the event was treated as "a highly contagious disease." Tissue samples "are currently being kept at Fort Detrick, Md.," and the AEC medical officer warned of inadequacy "[in dealing with] a large scale outbreak of the alien virus." This is the corpus's strongest statement that the EBEs were not just specimens but a **biohazard**.

### 5.4 SOM 1-01 contact procedure (`majestic-12/som101-part2.md`)

The manual anticipates *live* encounters: "Encounters initiated by EBEs … it is anticipated that encounters will take place at military installations … selected by mutual agreement," with the OPNAC Team preparing "cover stories for the press" if EBEs "land in public places without prior notice." This is the document that treats EBEs as potentially *interactive*, not merely dead specimens.

### 5.5 EME "field" material (`majestic-12/important-memo.md`)

There is no body-on-a-table report for EMEs. The memo's "field" claims are operational/historical rather than observational:
- MJ-12 concluded "the EBEs could gain control of U.S. defenses … by interfering with the guidance and targeting computers of the Atlas ICBMs."
- A claimed **1960 Eisenhower-approved** defense plan for a nuclear attack "through **EBE deception**," with retaliation withheld until "confirmation of UFO misidentification."
- Kennedy briefed during the Cuban Missile Crisis that "the **EBE deception** was the reason" for Soviet first-strike weapons.
- Successive briefings to Johnson and Nixon "on … the relationship with **EMEs** and how they influenced" U.S. affairs.

Note the memo uses **EBE** and **EME** in adjacent passages — the "deception" is attributed in places to EBEs and in places to EMEs — reflecting that this single, degraded document blurs the two even as it defines them separately.

## 8. Summary

The documents describe **two named non-human entity categories**:

1. **EBE — Extra-terrestrial Biological Entity:** physical beings, recovered as bodies, classified into Type I and Type II, autopsied, treated as a biohazard, and given a legal framework as a potential "people." Coined by Bronk's team on 30 November 1947; detailed in the Eisenhower Briefing, SOM 1-01, the IPU report, the Fifth Annual Report, and the Oppenheimer–Einstein draft.

2. **EME — Extraterrestrial Materialized Entity:** non-corporeal entities that "appear at will," ancient, deceptive, and framed as a psychological and spiritual threat that attacks the investigator's faith and sanity — closer to Jacques Vallée's interdimensional "control system" than to a spacecraft crew. Defined only in `important-memo`.

The essential distinction: **EBEs can be recovered, contained, and studied; EMEs cannot.** The corpus's field-report material is overwhelmingly on the EBE side — concrete body descriptions and autopsy/contamination accounts — while the EME is described in interpretive and warning terms rather than through any recovered specimen.

## Addendum A — EBOs and their soul-field cosmology (single narrative source)

> **Provenance caveat — read first.** Everything in this addendum comes from **one file**: `narratives/eboscientista-reddit-post-7-5-23.md`, an anonymous, first-person **testimonial Reddit post**. It is **not** part of the established government record — it does not appear in the Majestic-12 collection or in the Department of War / DoE / CIA / ODNI releases, and it is not corroborated by any document in those collections. It is included here only because readers (reasonably) ask how EBO relates to EBE/EME. Treat it as a narrative account, distinct in kind from the primary sources cited above. The author himself closes the relevant passage with: "I'm speaking from memory of something I read more than 10 years ago."

### What EBO is

**EBO — "Exo-Biospheric-Organisms"** — is the *author's own coinage* in the post, describing four incomplete bodies he claims were studied at a national-security lab (he names Fort Detrick / Battelle National Biodefense Institute). The described biology is detailed (DNA-based, 16 circular chromosomes, a "Tri-Palindromic Region," grey "biosynthetic film" skin, copper-rich blood). In its physical framing, EBO sits closest to the **EBE** thread — a recovered, studied, biological entity.

### Is there a "spiritual companion" to EBO, like EME is to EBE?

**No — not as a separate entity.** The EBE/EME pair locates the "spiritual" in a *second, distinct kind of being* (the non-corporeal EME). The EBO narrative does something structurally different: it places the spiritual dimension **inside the EBOs themselves**, as their *religion / cosmology*, not as a paired entity category. There is no "E\*E"-style spiritual acronym anywhere in the EBO source.

### The EBO soul-field belief (as described)

Per the post's Q&A on "their religion":

- **The soul is a field, like gravity.** "EBOs believe that the soul is not an extension of the individual, but rather a fundamental characteristic of nature that expresses itself as a [f]ield, not unlike gravity."
- **Life raises its complexity.** In the presence of life the field "acquires complexity, resulting in negative entropy," correlated with the concentration of living organisms; over time this yields sentient life, through which "the [f]ield begins to express itself … forming what we call the soul," in a "positive feedback loop."
- **Apotheosis is the goal.** "When the [f]ield reaches a 'critical mass', there will be a sort of apotheosis … this quest for apotheosis seems to be the EBOs[’] main motivation."
- **Held as fact, not faith.** Per the author's appendix, for the EBOs "the soul [f]ield is not a belief but an obvious truth"; "the soul loses its individuality after death, but … memory and experience persist as part of the [f]ield," producing "a society that doesn't fear death but which places no importance or reverence on individuality."
- **The motive behind their conduct.** This "compels them to seed life, shape it, nurture it, monitor it and influence it for the ultimate purpose of creating this apotheosis. Paradoxically, they have little or no respect for an individual's well-being."

### How the corpus locates "the spiritual" — two models

| Physical entity | Where the "spiritual" lives | Source class |
|---|---|---|
| **EBE** | A *separate* non-corporeal entity — the **EME** | MJ-12 collection (primary record) |
| **EBO** | *No separate entity* — an internal **soul-field cosmology** the EBOs follow | Single narrative Reddit post (testimonial) |

So the answer to "is there a spiritual companion to EBO like EME?" is: the spiritual idea is present, but the corpus expresses it two different ways — as a **separate entity** in the established documents (EME), and as an **entity's own internal cosmology** in the single EBO narrative. The two should not be merged: one is the established record, the other is an uncorroborated first-person account.
